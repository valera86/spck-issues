Our use of cookies and tracking

Cookies

Spck Editor uses cookies and similar web browser technologies, like local storage and IndexedDB for storing settings as well as to provide you with our Services.

Cookies are small text files that websites often store on computer hard drives or mobile devices of visitors. We use cookies to provide you our services, for example, to keep you logged in, remember your preferences, identify your device for security purposes, and provide information for future development of Spck Editor. By using our Website, you agree that we can place these types of cookies on your computer or device. If you disable your browser or device’s ability to accept these cookies, you will not be able to log in or use our Services.

Another browser technology we use to store content, code, graphics, fonts, text, or other information is called IndexedDB. This provides long term persistent storage of such data on your computer.

You can adjust your browser settings to block cookies or access to local storage. Some features of the Site will not work if cookies are blocked.

Cloudflare

Cloudflare is a traffic optimization and distribution service provided by Cloudflare Inc. The way Cloudflare is integrated means that it filters all the traffic through our Site, i.e., communication between our Site and the User’s browser, while also allowing analytical data from this Application to be collected.

This type of service allows this Application to distribute their content using servers located across different countries and to optimize their performance. Considering the widespread distribution of this system, it is difficult to determine the locations to which the contents that may contain Personal Information User are transferred.
